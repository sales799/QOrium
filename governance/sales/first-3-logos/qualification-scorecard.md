# Qualification Scorecard — BANT-equivalent

A 5-dimension scorecard run during/after every discovery call. Total
out of 25; threshold ≥ 18 to advance to demo. ≥ 22 to advance to
pilot proposal.

---

## Dimension 1 — Pain Awareness (5 points)

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| 5 | Customer specifically describes leakage/cheating/false-pass/audit pain in their first 5 sentences |
| 4 | Customer agrees with pain framing, gives specific recent example |
| 3 | Customer acknowledges pain but treats it as one of many |
| 2 | Customer says "we'll worry about it later" |
| 1 | Customer is unaware OR doesn't agree |

---

## Dimension 2 — Champion Quality (5 points)

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| 5 | Direct decision-maker on the call (CEO / VP Eng / CTO); has personal pain |
| 4 | Strong champion (Head of TA / Director Eng); will socialise internally |
| 3 | Influencer (senior IC / Engineering Manager); needs help reaching DM |
| 2 | Junior contact; cannot drive deal |
| 1 | Wrong-altitude (procurement / vendor manager only) |

---

## Dimension 3 — Technical Fit (5 points)

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| 5 | Already runs HackerRank / Mettl / Codility AND complaining about it |
| 4 | Already runs assessment SaaS; open to vendor change |
| 3 | DIY tech screen; ready to outsource |
| 2 | DIY tech screen; not ready to outsource |
| 1 | No tech screen at all |

---

## Dimension 4 — Budget Signal (5 points)

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| 5 | Recent funding (last 12 months) AND has explicit assessment-tooling line item |
| 4 | Has assessment line item; renewal in next 6 months |
| 3 | Has discretionary budget; needs internal sponsorship |
| 2 | No line item; would need to create new budget |
| 1 | Recent layoffs / freeze / PE-driven cost-cut |

---

## Dimension 5 — Decision Speed (5 points)

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| 5 | 1-step approval (CEO/founder); commits in 4-8 weeks |
| 4 | 2-step (CEO + CFO); commits in 8-12 weeks |
| 3 | Committee + procurement; 12-20 weeks |
| 2 | Annual budget cycle (next FY); ≥ 6 months |
| 1 | Indefinite committee; > 12 months |

---

## Total + decision rule

| Total | Action |
|---|---|
| 22-25 | **Advance to pilot proposal** within 1 week |
| 18-21 | Advance to second demo + ROI sheet; pilot in 2-3 weeks |
| 14-17 | Stay in nurture; don't burn AE cycles |
| ≤ 13 | Polite decline; move to 6-month re-engage |

---

## Mandatory floors

- D1 (Pain Awareness) ≥ 3 — without pain, there is no urgency
- D2 (Champion) ≥ 3 — without a champion, the deal stalls
- D4 (Budget) ≥ 2 — score 1 is auto-disqualify

---

## Anti-patterns

- **The forever-pilot prospect** — high D1, D2, D3 but D5 = 1.
  Politely decline; this becomes a 12-month resource drain.
- **The "free customer"** — score 4-5 across D1-D3 but D4 = 1.
  Often these are early-stage HRTechs (ICP-3); offer a co-marketing
  / case-study deal at heavy discount; do NOT do it free (we
  did Talpro free as Customer-Zero; we don't do free again).
- **The procurement-led inquiry** — low D2 + low D5. The customer's
  procurement reached out first; champion doesn't yet exist
  internally. Decline politely; ask procurement to introduce the
  technical champion.
- **The "evaluate every vendor" exec** — high D2, D3, D4 but D1 = 2.
  They're tire-kicking. Decline OR force a single-page comparison
  exercise + 4-week decision deadline.

---

## Use in workflow

1. **End of discovery call:** AE writes scorecard immediately
2. **Within 24 hours:** scorecard logged in CRM with
   per-dimension rationale
3. **Weekly retro:** CEO + AE review all new scorecards;
   top opportunities advance
4. **Monthly:** scorecard distributions reviewed; calibration
   adjusted if drift detected

---

## Calibration

After first 10 discovery calls, run a calibration:
- Did high-scoring opportunities convert?
- Did low-scoring opportunities reactivate?

Adjust dimension weighting if signal is off. v0 weighting is
1:1:1:1:1; we may shift to (1.5:1.0:1.0:1.5:1.0) if Pain + Budget
prove more predictive than Champion + Decision Speed.
