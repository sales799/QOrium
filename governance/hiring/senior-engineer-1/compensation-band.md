## ESOP details

- **Total grant:** 0.5-1.0% of fully-diluted post-Series-A cap table
- **Vest:** 4 years · 1-year cliff · 25% at year 1, monthly accrual
  thereafter
- **Strike price:** at FMV at grant date
- **Acceleration:** single-trigger acceleration on change-of-control
  for 12 months of unvested
- **Post-employment exercise window:** 90 days standard;
  extendable to 12 months for outstanding performers
- **Tax handling:** India ESOP 2-stage taxation; QOrium provides
  counsel briefing at grant
- **Optional:** at year 2-3, eligible for an ESOP refresh grant
  per CEO + CTO discretion; refresh band 0.2-0.4%

## Variable comp

For Y1: **no variable**. Reasons:
- Avoids perverse incentives on a quality-gate role
- Y1 cash conservation: no bonus accrual
- Senior IC value is best assessed annually

For Y2: 10-15% target bonus tied to:
- Sprint velocity sustained
- Production uptime targets met
- Customer-bound feature ship rate
- 1-2 mentorship hires landed

| Counter | Response |

| Within ±5% of band | Accept; close |
| +5% to +15% | Offer +10% in exchange for 12-month commit OR additional ESOP at lower base |
| +15% to +30% | Decline politely; "we anchor at this band; happy to revisit at year-1 review" |
| > +30% | Decline; not the right fit at this stage |
| Cash-heavy | Decline; equity participation is structural for first non-founder engineer |
| ESOP-heavy "halve cash, double ESOP" | Discuss; willing on case-by-case if candidate has cash runway |
| Returning India-diaspora needs relocation | Add ₹5-10L relocation budget on top; one-time; reportable |

**Signing bonus:** up to ₹10L on a 12-month clawback. Use for
candidates pulling out of competing offers OR returning India-
diaspora.

bidding wars.
