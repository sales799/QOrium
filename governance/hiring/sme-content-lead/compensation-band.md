## ESOP details

- **Total grant:** 0.4-0.8% of fully-diluted post-Series-A cap table
  (depends on tier; see table above)
- **Vest schedule:** 4 years with a 1-year cliff (industry standard
  in India tech); 25% at year 1, then monthly accrual
- **Strike price:** at fair market value at grant date (per latest
  valuation; if pre-Series-A, FMV is per a Black-Scholes model
  reviewed by counsel)
- **Acceleration:** single-trigger acceleration on change-of-control
  for 12 months of unvested portion (standard); double-trigger
  available on individual negotiation
- **Post-employment exercise window:** 90 days from termination
  (industry standard); we'd consider extending to 12 months for
  outstanding performers per CEO discretion
- **Tax handling:** India ESOP taxation is 2-stage (perquisite at
  exercise + capital gains at sale); QOrium provides a tax counsel
  briefing at grant

## Variable comp / bonuses

For Y1, **no variable component**. Reasons:

- The role's impact is best judged annually, not quarterly
- Paying variable to a quality-gate role creates perverse incentive
  ("ship more questions to hit numbers")
- Y1 cash conservation: variable comp inflates Y1 burn

For Y2: revisit with a 10-15% target bonus tied to:
- Wave-3 ramp completion
- Library size targets
- Public methodology white paper authorship
- Team hire success

## Negotiation guidance

| Counter | Response |
|---|---|
| Within ±5% of band | Accept; close the offer |
| +5% to +15% | Offer +10% in exchange for an explicit 12-month commitment OR additional ESOP at lower base |
| +15% to +30% | Decline politely; "we anchor at this band; happy to revisit at year 1 review" |
| > +30% | Decline; not the right candidate at this stage |
| Cash-heavy: "no ESOP, all cash" | Decline. The role's prestige + future-CDO-track depends on equity participation |
| ESOP-heavy: "halve cash, double ESOP" | Decline at hire. Y1 needs cash sufficiency for the candidate's life; we can revisit ratio at year-2 review |

**Signing bonus:** up to ₹5L on a 12-month clawback (returnable if
the hire leaves before year 1). Use sparingly; not for everyone.

**Counter-offer protection:** if the candidate has a competing offer,
match-or-decline within 7 days. Do not enter bidding wars.

  the Y2 budget is self-funding

CEO judgment call: hire the right SME Lead 4-6 months earlier at the
cost of an external bridge round, OR wait for revenue + hire 4-6
months later. Recommendation: **wait** unless a once-in-a-cycle
candidate appears.
