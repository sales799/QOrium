## Why this is a Year-1 FTE not a contractor

- **Scope is open-ended.** Quality validation is not a 6-month bounded
  project; it's continuous as long as new items are authored.
- **Internal context.** The SME Lead participates in roadmap, hires
  authors below them, runs the Wave-3 review queue daily.
- **Brand stake.** Their name appears on the customer + investor
  Trust page; that's an FTE-level commitment.
- **ESOP signal.** Top-tier SME leads choose between offers; an
  early-stage equity grant beats a senior consulting retainer for
  attracting the right person.
