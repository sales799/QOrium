explicit CEO approval per Auto-Mode-Remote-Plan-v1 stop conditions.

Four templates cover the four most common acquisition channels:

1. **Cold email** — direct outreach to academic + applied psychometrics
   community
2. **Warm intro request** — to a mutual contact who can intro
3. **LinkedIn DM** — short-form for LinkedIn-first outreach
4. **Follow-up #1 / #2** — gentle nudge templates

## Sourcing pipeline (recommended)

Before sending any of these, build a list of 10–15 candidates from:

- **Indian I/O psych community:** Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS),
  IIT-Bombay HSS dept, IIM-Bangalore OB+HR, Delhi Univ Psychology,
  XLRI-Jamshedpur OB
- **Applied practice:** SHL India psychometricians (post-3-year tenure),
  ex-Mettl/Mercer senior IC, ex-NIIT psychometric consultants
- **Academic-applied bridge:** Indian Society of Industrial &
  Organizational Psychology (ISIOP) members; APA Division 14 Indian-
  diaspora members
- **LinkedIn search filters:**
  - Title: "Industrial Psychologist" OR "Psychometrician" OR "Test
    Development Specialist" OR "Assessment Director"
  - Industry: HR Services, Higher Education, Computer Software
  - Location: India OR India-diaspora (US/UK/Singapore)
  - 2nd-degree connections preferred

For each candidate, capture:
- Name · institution · email · LinkedIn URL · 1-line "why them"
- Track in a Google Sheet (or `governance/hiring/io-psych-contractor/candidate-tracker.csv` once permitted)

## Template 1 — Cold Email

**Subject:** I/O Psychometrics Consulting — QOrium ReadyBank
(IRT-calibrated assessment library)

> Dear Dr. [Last Name],
>
> I'm Bhaskar Anand, founder of QOrium Technologies. We're building
> ReadyBank, an IRT-calibrated, anti-leak-rotated, watermark-per-
> candidate question library for technical hiring — currently 1,300
> released items across Java, Python, AWS, SAP-ABAP, Oracle HCM, and
> seven other domains.
>
> We're now opening Wave-3: 200 psychometric items (aptitude,
> personality, situational judgement). Our engineering team has built
> the IRT calibration pipeline (2PL/3PL Birnbaum + JMLE + Mantel-
> Haenszel DIF, in production). What we don't have, and what we
> won't ship without, is credentialed I/O psychology signoff on the
> methodology and the items themselves.
>
> I'd like to engage an experienced I/O psychologist as a 6-month
> contractor (10–15 hours per week, asynchronous, output-based) to:
>
> – Sign off on our Wave-3 authoring template + bias-detection methodology
> – Co-author the Reference Panel Governance Charter (~200 respondents)
> – Validate the calibration outputs once Panel responses arrive
> – Co-author a public methodology white paper
>
> Compensation is milestone-tied; full SOW available on request.
>
> If this fits your practice (or if you can suggest a colleague who'd
> be a better match), I'd welcome a 30-minute call this or next week.
>
> Background: I'm a third-time founder (Talpro, Hindustan Recruitment,
> QOrium), ex-IIT engineering-ops, currently CEO+CTO of QOrium with a
> Year-1 budget allocation in place.
>
> Thank you for your time,
> Bhaskar Anand
> CEO, QOrium Technologies
> https://qorium.online
> [email signature block]

**Length:** 220 words. Within the 250-word soft cap academic readers
expect for unsolicited consulting outreach.

**Personalisation hooks** (replace before sending each instance):
- Specific paper of theirs you've read OR specific institution context
- Reference to a shared context (academic society, conference)
- Reason they specifically (e.g., "your 2022 paper on DIF in technical
  assessments stood out")

## Sending discipline

- **Stagger sends** — no more than 5 per day to avoid spam-filter
  pattern detection
- **Personalise every email** — at minimum, the salutation and one
  sentence reference to the recipient's work
- **Track responses in `candidate-tracker.csv`** — status: sent,
  bounced, declined, scheduled, signed
- **First-call discipline** — when one accepts, schedule the call
  within 5 business days; pre-share the SOW PDF
- **No mass mail** — these are individual outreaches; keep the From
  address as the CEO's personal QOrium address, never a noreply

## What to do if no one accepts after 15 sends

Per Auto-Mode-Remote-Plan stop condition, escalate to CTO Office for
a Phase 2 sourcing strategy:

- Engage a specialist recruiter (15-20% placement fee on a 6-month
  engagement is typical)
- Open a public RFP on Indian psychometric society mailing lists
- Consider raising the retainer band by 20% to widen the pool
