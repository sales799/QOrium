# Outreach Channels — Reference Panel Recruitment

**Goal:** ≥200 completed sessions across all sub-skill cohorts in
8 weeks. **Channel mix:** 3 channels weighted by cost-per-completed-
session and cohort balance contribution.

## Channel 1 — Campus partnerships

**Targets (Tier-1 engineering schools, India):**

- IITs (Bombay, Delhi, Madras, Kanpur, Kharagpur — Tier-1 6)
- IIITs (Hyderabad, Bangalore, Allahabad)
- BITS Pilani / Goa / Hyderabad
- NITs (Trichy, Surathkal, Warangal)
- Top private (VIT, Manipal, SRM, COEP, PESIT, RVCE)
- TISS (for AIPE / non-CS technical roles)

**Approach:**

1. **Career services partnership** — formal letter to placement
   cells: "QOrium is offering a paid skills assessment honorarium
   for students; cohort balance to inform IRT calibration; ₹2,000
   per 90-min session." Most placement cells will accept; some need
   institutional signoff.
2. **Student club partnerships** — Coding Club / GDSC / ACM chapters
   often co-host 30-min info sessions; QOrium provides slides + Q&A
   + bulk panel-token codes
3. **Faculty channel** — for AIPE, embedded automotive, OHCM (the
   "less obvious" sub-skills), reach out to relevant department
   chairs offering co-publication credit on the methodology white
   paper

**Cohort contribution:**
- Strong on 0-1 + 1-3 year experience bands
- Strong on Wave-1 sub-skills (Java, Python, React, SQL, AWS, AIPE)
- Weaker on 7+ year experience bands (need professional channel)
- Weaker on Wave-2 (SAP-ABAP, OHCM, CPQ, Finacle, Embedded
  Automotive — these need professional + community channel)

**Cadence:**
- Week 1-2: outreach to top 12 institutions; book 6-8 confirmed
- Week 3-6: actual session campaigns; expect 10-20 completed per
  campaign

**Budget breakdown (₹1.5-2L):**
- Travel + accommodation for 4 in-person visits: ₹60K
- Online session ops (Zoom, swag, email coordination): ₹20K
- Faculty channel: white-paper-credit value (non-cash)
- Reserve: ₹50-70K for surprise costs (reschedules, extra
  honorariums)

## Anti-fraud + attention checks

Reference Panel data has only one purpose: real human responses for
IRT calibration. Survey-farmer fraud kills the calibration.

Defenses:

- **Attention-check items (3 per assessment):** trivial-correct
  questions designed to fail randomly-clicking respondents
- **Time-on-question filter:** items completed in < 10s flagged
- **Duplicate detection:** same answer pattern across 5+ items =
  flag
- **IP / device fingerprint anomaly detection:** same fingerprint
  filling > 5 panel-tokens = flag (even if different emails)
- **Email-domain throwaway blacklist:** mailinator etc. auto-rejected
- **Manual-review queue:** all flagged sessions reviewed by CDO
  Office Y1 + I/O Psych contractor before payment

If a session fails attention-checks, we pay ₹500 honorarium
(participation-fee) and exclude from calibration. No silent
no-payment.
