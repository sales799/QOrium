## §6 — Send Discipline (apply to all CEO outbound)

- **Tuesday-Thursday 9-11 AM IST or 6-8 PM IST** is the optimal sender window for Indian B2B
- **Avoid month-end-close week** (Bosch CFOs and similar are busy)
- **Never BCC anyone** on warm-intro emails (looks transactional)
- **Personalize** the [Counsel name] / [CFO name] / [Contact name] before sending
- **Phone number in signature** — adds legitimacy for Indian counterparties
