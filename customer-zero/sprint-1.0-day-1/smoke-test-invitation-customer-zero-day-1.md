## Send mechanism (Day-1)

**For the very first candidate (Day-1):**
- Manual send from `hiring@talpro.in` to a single named candidate, after Talpro Delivery Head named.
- Avoid bulk-mailer routing for the first send (Mailgun/Sendgrid bulk inboxes risk spam folder; first impression matters).
- Single-use session URL must be pre-generated and tested end-to-end before sending.

**From Day-2 onward:**
- Talpro Delivery Head can mail-merge from CRM (HubSpot or Mailchimp tied to Talpro brand).
- All sends are recorded in `customer_zero_feedback` with `invitation_sent_at` timestamp.

## Compliance notes

1. **Consent:** Talpro India's existing candidate privacy policy already covers internal screening tools. No additional consent is required for Day-1.
2. **Data residency:** candidate response data stays in Indian-region storage (Hostinger VPS Bengaluru region OR Mac Mini Bangalore). No cross-border transfer pre-Pre-A.
3. **DPA:** A7 DPA v0.1 (drafted Run #2) governs the QOrium↔Talpro data flow internally; signed by both CEO + CTO once K&S Partners reply.
4. **Right to opt out:** candidate can decline by replying with "I prefer not to take the assessment" — recorded as `declined_by_candidate` in CRM, **never** counted as fail.
5. **Right to retake:** if technical issue (browser crash, connection drop), candidate can re-take once within 48 hours; this is the explicit policy and is mentioned in the FAQ.
