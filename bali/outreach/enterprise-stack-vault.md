## Discovery call — agenda (45 minutes)

1. **Their hiring landscape** (10 min) — annual volume, role mix, which stacks bleed candidates the most, where leaks have hurt them publicly or privately.
2. **The Stack-Vault concept** (10 min) — exclusive, watermarked, role-graph-tagged, quarterly refresh. Show the role-graph diagram from `qorium.online/product`.
3. **Customer Zero proof** (5 min) — Talpro India runs 50+ screens/week through QOrium on the same engine. Offer a 15-min reference call (SO-12).
4. **Anti-leak engine** (5 min) — daily rotation, watermark forensics. Reference `qorium.online/security` for the data flow.
5. **Pricing anchor** (5 min) — ₹40L/year standard scope; 3-year terms available with renewal discounts (5-10%) tied to references per SO-12.
6. **Procurement + security** (5 min) — DPDPA-ready, GDPR-ready, SOC 2 in-progress (per `/security` page — never claim Type II we don't have).
7. **Next step** (5 min) — 2-page scoping memo within 5 business days, then a follow-up call to align on scope.

**Closing line:** _"Stack-Vault is contractually exclusive — we never re-sell a question that's in your vault. The watermark forensics make that enforceable, not just promised."_

## Objection handling (cross-reference Bali Playbook §8)

**O1 — "We'll just buy ReadyBank, why pay 20× for Stack-Vault?"**
_Answer:_ ReadyBank is shared. Stack-Vault is contractually exclusive — your library never appears in any shared product or any other customer's Stack-Vault. The watermark forensics make exclusivity enforceable. For enterprise hiring at GCC scale, exclusivity is the difference between a library that holds and one that's on Reddit Friday.

**O2 — "Bosch procurement is 6+ months. We can't wait."**
_Answer:_ Standard for enterprise. We start with a 2-page scoping memo, MSA + DPA in parallel, and library delivery 8 weeks post-contract. We've held this timeline through 6-month procurement at GCC scale.

**O5 — "Pricing pushback — your floor is too high."**
_Answer:_ The floor exists for a reason — Stack-Vault is contractually exclusive. Lower than ₹35L means we can't sustain the exclusivity guarantee. I can offer multi-year terms (3-year at ₹38L/year average) or a phased delivery, but the floor holds. (SO-11 — CEO approval required to go below.)

**O6 — "We need SOC 2 Type II before we sign."**
_Answer:_ SOC 2 Type II is in progress per our roadmap (target M21). We can scope the contract under DPDPA-ready + GDPR-ready posture today, sign DPA, and add a SOC 2 attestation rider for Year 2 trigger when Type II completes. No false claims — `qorium.online/security` shows our exact posture.

**O8 — "Talpro India is our competitor — why would we buy from your sister company?"**
_Answer:_ Fair concern. Talpro is Customer Zero — meaning they're a customer, not a competitor in the question-supply business. QOrium and Talpro are sister companies in the Talpro Universe with non-compete on question supply. Your Stack-Vault is exclusive to you per SO-10 — Talpro never sees it. Want to talk to {{prospect_in_a_similar_position}} who already worked through this?
