# Bali Day 0-30 Onboarding — AE Enterprise / BD Platforms

**Audience:** First Bali Sales Office hires (AE Enterprise + BD Platforms, target M3 per Bali Playbook §2)
**Owner of program:** CEO (Y1) → Sales Lead (Y2+)
**Time commitment:** ~25 hours over 30 days alongside live ramp

---

## Why this exists

The Bali Sales Office charter (Constitution §2.7) is dense. The Bali Sales Playbook is 16 sections. The operating folder (`bali/`) is 9 files of templates, scripts, and rubrics. A new hire who's expected to be productive by Day 31 needs a structured path through it — not "read everything and good luck."

This document is that structured path.

---

If any of the above is missing on Day 1, flag immediately.

## Day 30 self-check

You should be able to answer YES to all of these:

- [ ] I can recite the locked USP from Constitution §1.1 verbatim.
- [ ] I can recite the 3 motions and their Y1 logo targets without looking.
- [ ] I can recite my motion's Stage 1-7 conversion benchmarks (`pipeline-tracker.md`).
- [ ] I have ≥10 active opportunities in CRM in my motion.
- [ ] I have closed-won 0 deals (expected — cycle is 1-12 months) BUT I have ≥1 stage-3-or-later opportunity advancing.
- [ ] I have presented in 4 Mon weekly forecasts and 4 Fri weekly debriefs.
- [ ] I have presented in 1 monthly business review.
- [ ] I know which escalations go to CEO (>10% discount, <₹35L Stack-Vault, DPC engagement, MANTHAN trigger).
- [ ] I know the §2.7 outreach discipline (forbidden / CEO-escalation / allowed classes).
- [ ] I have NEVER fabricated a customer logo, ARR number, or feature claim (SO-24).
- [ ] I have ALWAYS asked before quoting Talpro India as a reference (SO-12).

If any answer is NO, re-walk the relevant section with CEO before Day 31.

## What's NOT in the first 30 days (deferred to Day 31+)

These are real responsibilities but they're stage-2 of onboarding (Days 31-90):

- ❌ Running a quarterly competitive scan (next due 2026-08-05; observe-only Y1 first time)
- ❌ Managing a closed-won contract through procurement + legal end-to-end (typically Y1 month 4-6)
- ❌ Owning the Bali AI Agent's escalation queue (humans rotate; you start observing M3+)
- ❌ Independently authorizing pricing exceptions in the 5-10% band (CEO authorizes Y1 unless explicit delegation)
- ❌ Closing a Stack-Vault deal solo (CEO is on every Stack-Vault contract Y1 — SO-1 + SO-11 dictate)
