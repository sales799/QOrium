## 1. Who we are

QOrium is a Question-Bank-as-a-Service tech-hiring assessment platform operated by Talpro India Pvt Ltd. We sell the platform to recruiter organisations who use it to assess software engineering candidates they are hiring.

Two categories of users interact with the platform:

- **Recruiter Customers** — paying recruiter organisations (the data controller for their hiring workflow)
- **Candidates** — software engineering candidates invited by a Recruiter Customer to take an assessment (data subjects)

This notice covers how we handle Candidate personal data. Recruiter Customer agreements are covered separately in commercial contracts.

---

## 2. What personal data we collect from Candidates

| Category             | Examples                                                | Source                                       | Purpose                                             |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Identity             | First name, last name, email                            | Recruiter Customer who invited the candidate | Address the candidate; route results                |
| Authentication       | Login OTP codes (transient), session tokens             | Generated by us                              | Account security                                    |
| Assessment responses | Answers to questions, time-on-task, language preference | Candidate enters during assessment           | Compute score                                       |
| Device + technical   | IP address, browser user-agent, screen resolution       | HTTP headers                                 | Anti-fraud (multi-attempt detection); accessibility |
| Watermark seed       | Per-render unique HMAC seed                             | Generated by us                              | Anti-leak attribution if questions appear publicly  |
| Optional demographic | Years of experience, current role, city                 | If candidate fills profile                   | Calibration math (Reference Panel members only)     |

**We do NOT collect:** Aadhaar, PAN, social media handles, biometric data, browsing history outside our platform, contacts/address book, or location beyond IP-based country.

---

## 3. Legal basis (DPDPA 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023:

- **Consent** — Candidate accepts this notice before starting an assessment (positive opt-in via take-flow consent screen).
- **Legitimate use** — fraud detection, anti-leak attribution, account security.
- **Contractual necessity** — fulfilling the assessment commissioned by the Recruiter Customer.

## 5. Who we share data with

- **The Recruiter Customer who invited you** — the platform exists for them to see your result
- **Cloud infrastructure providers under processor agreements:**
  - AWS (compute, storage, email send via SES; ap-south-1 Mumbai region)
  - Cloudflare (DNS, WAF, CDN; global edge with India POP)
  - Hostinger (Origin VPS hosting)
- **Anthropic** — limited Claude API calls for JD-Forge generation (no Candidate PII passes through; only anonymised question content)
- **Statutory authorities** — only on lawful order; we publish a transparency log post-fact (annual)

We do NOT sell, rent, or share Candidate data with advertisers, social networks, or unrelated third parties.

---

## 6. Where data is stored

Primary: AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai). DR: AWS ap-southeast-1 (Singapore) for backup-only; no live traffic.

Data sovereignty: Candidate data of Indian residents stays in India for primary serving; cross-border replication for backup only is permitted under DPDPA §17.

## 7. Your rights as a Candidate (DPDPA §11-§14)

You can:

- **Access** — request a copy of your personal data we hold (free, within 30 days)
- **Correct** — fix inaccuracies (we'll update within 7 days of verification)
- **Erase** — request deletion (30-day completion; some retention may be required by SO-21 audit law)
- **Withdraw consent** — stop further processing (existing assessment results stay with Recruiter who commissioned)
- **Grievance** — escalate complaints to our Data Protection Officer (below) or directly to the Data Protection Board of India under DPDPA §32

How to exercise: email **privacy@qorium.online** with subject "DPDPA Request: <type>".

## 9. Security

- Encryption in transit: TLS 1.2+ everywhere (Cloudflare-managed certificates)
- Encryption at rest: AWS-managed KMS keys per-region; per-vault pepper rotation for Stack-Vault tenants
- Authentication: argon2id password hashing for Recruiters; OTP for Candidates
- Audit logging: 30-day hot, 1-year warm, 7-year cold (per Constitution §10.3)
- Bug bounty: not yet open; planned for M9. Responsible disclosure to security@qorium.online

---

## 10. Children's data

QOrium is for adult software-engineering hiring. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If a Candidate identifies as a minor, the assessment is voided and data deleted within 72 hours.

---

## 11. Cookies

We use only strictly-necessary cookies (session tokens, anti-CSRF). No advertising, analytics, or behavioural-tracking cookies. Marketing apex (qorium.online) is cookieless.
